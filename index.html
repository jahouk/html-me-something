About Me

   My name is Jason Houk and I've always been a bit of a foodie. One of my goals has always been to open my own coffee shop whether that was as part of a bakery or some other concept.

   A little more about me, I stumbled into computers while living in Southern Oregon and had a good run doing technical support. While in Southern Oregon I worked at a call center where I did support for clients such as Disney Interactive, Packard Bell Premiere Support, Apple, and Sonic Solutions.

   I would later go on to move to the St. Louis area and continue my career in IT. In St. Louis I worked for the Zippatoni Company and Clark Refining and Marketing as a temp before landing a permanent job at Enterprise Rent-A-Car. And following that I moved on to McKendree College, running thier helpdesk.

   My IT career ended in 2002 when McKendree went through a round of layoffs and I did not have the college degree to get past HR. I say all that to give my technical background as I return to talking about food. In 2013 after not being able to find anything in IT, I decided to go to culinary school at Southwestern Illinois College.

   After greduating from culinary school I worked for Peel Pizza in both thier Edwardsville and O'Fallon, IL locations. I was there when the O'Fallon location opened but left in December when I lost a toe due to complications from diabetes.
